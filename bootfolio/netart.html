Net Art

This website consists of a short series of liminal scenes from an old game series, the Deep Sleep Trilogy (2012-2014). The scenes have intentionally been removed from their chronological order in the game, and have been selectively picked to help establish a sense of familiarity and uncanniness that often comes with liminal space.
